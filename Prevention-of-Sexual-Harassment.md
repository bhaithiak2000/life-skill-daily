# Prevention of Sexual Harassment (PoSH)

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

Sexual harassment includes any unwelcome behaviour of a sexual nature that makes a person feel uncomfortable, unsafe, or offended. Examples include:

- Unwanted physical contact (touching, patting, hugging, brushing against someone)
- Sexually suggestive comments, jokes, or remarks about appearance or body
- Sending or showing sexual messages, images, or videos
- Repeated requests for dates or personal interaction despite refusal
- Staring, leering, or making gestures of a sexual nature
- Asking intrusive questions about someone’s personal or sexual life
- Threats, intimidation, or promises of benefits in exchange for sexual favours
- Creating a hostile or uncomfortable work environment through sexual behaviour

Such behaviour can be verbal, non-verbal, physical, written, or online (messages, emails, social media, etc.).

---

## 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

If I face or witness sexual harassment, I would take the following steps:

1. **Recognize and Acknowledge**  
   Understand that the behaviour is inappropriate and not acceptable.

2. **Communicate Clearly (if safe)**  
   Tell the person that their behaviour is unwelcome and ask them to stop.

3. **Document the Incident**  
   Note dates, times, location, what happened, and any witnesses. Save messages or evidence.

4. **Report Through Proper Channels**  
   Report the incident to:
   - Reporting Manager, or
   - HR / PoSH Committee (Internal Complaints Committee – ICC), as per MountBlue policy.

5. **Support the Affected Person (if witness)**  
   Encourage them to report, offer to accompany them, and maintain confidentiality.

6. **Cooperate with Investigation**  
   Provide truthful information if an inquiry is conducted.

7. **Ensure Personal Safety**  
   Avoid isolated interaction with the offender and seek immediate help if necessary.

I would follow MountBlue’s PoSH policy and procedures to ensure a safe, respectful, and inclusive workplace.
