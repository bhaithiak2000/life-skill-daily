## Question 1: Activities that make me relax - Calm quadrant?
- Listening to soft music  
- Taking a short walk outside  
- Sitting quietly without phone or screen  
- Light stretching or yoga  
- Talking with close friends or family  
- Watching something light or funny  
- Deep breathing for a few minutes
