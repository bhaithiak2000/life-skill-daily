# Grit and Growth Mindset

## What is Grit?
- Grit is the ability to stay committed to long-term goals
- It means not giving up when things get difficult
- It combines passion and perseverance

## Why Grit is Important
- Success takes time
- Skills improve through consistent effort
- Failures are part of learning

## What is Growth Mindset?
- Belief that abilities can improve with effort
- Mistakes are opportunities to learn
- Intelligence is not fixed

## Fixed vs Growth Mindset
- Fixed mindset – "I am not good at this"
- Growth mindset – "I can improve with practice"

## How I Can Develop Grit
- Set long-term goals
- Practice daily
- Accept failures calmly
- Stay consistent

## How I Can Develop Growth Mindset
- Learn from feedback
- Replace negative self-talk
- Focus on progress
- Keep challenging myself

## Conclusion
- Grit helps me stay consistent
- Growth mindset helps me improve
- Both are essential for success
