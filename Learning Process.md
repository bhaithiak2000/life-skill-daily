# Learning Process – Q&A

## Question 1: What is the Feynman Technique? (1 line)

- The Feynman Technique is a learning method where you understand a concept deeply by explaining it in simple words as if teaching a beginner.

---

## Question 2: Most Interesting Idea from Barbara Oakley’s TED Talk

- The idea that the brain uses two modes of thinking: focused mode and diffused mode.
- Learning is more effective when we switch between intense focus and relaxed thinking.
- Struggling with a problem is normal; taking breaks actually helps the brain find solutions.
- The story showing that even people who think they are “bad at math” can improve by changing how they learn.

---

## Question 3: What are Active (Focused) and Diffused Modes of Thinking?

**Focused Mode:**
- Used when concentrating hard on a task  
- Logical, step-by-step thinking  
- Useful for solving specific problems  
- Happens when attention is fully directed  

**Diffused Mode:**
- Happens when the mind is relaxed  
- Makes broader connections  
- Helps in creativity and big-picture understanding  
- Occurs during rest, walking, or showering  

---

## Question 4: Steps to Take When Learning Something New (Learn Anything in 20 Hours)

- Break the skill into smaller sub-skills  
- Learn enough to self-correct  
- Remove distractions during practice  
- Practice at least 20 hours with focus  

---

## Question 5: Actions to Improve My Learning Process

- Work with full attention and avoid multitasking  
- Keep phone on silent and remove unnecessary notifications  
- Block social media during study/work time  
- Follow “work when you work, play when you play” rule  
- Track time spent on learning  
- Practice deep work (long focused sessions)  
- Focus on understanding concepts, not just memorizing  
- Try to explain concepts in my own words  
- Implement concepts through coding practice  
- Set deadlines and complete tasks on time  
- Take short breaks to allow diffused thinking  
- Make learning enjoyable and curiosity-driven  
- Communicate clearly when facing difficulties  
- Aim to master a few important skills deeply
