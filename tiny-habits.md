# Tiny Habits

## What are Tiny Habits?
- Small actions that are easy to do
- Take less than 1 minute
- Help build consistency

## Why Tiny Habits Work
- Easy to start
- No motivation required
- Reduce procrastination
- Build confidence slowly

## Habit Formula
- After I [existing habit], I will [tiny habit]
- Example: After I brush my teeth, I will do 5 push-ups

## How to Build Tiny Habits
- Start very small
- Attach habit to an existing routine
- Celebrate small wins
- Increase gradually

## Challenges in Building Habits
- Expecting fast results
- Trying too much at once
- Lack of consistency

## How I Can Apply Tiny Habits
- Study 5 minutes daily
- Read 1 page per day
- Write 5 lines of code practice daily
- Review notes every night

## Conclusion
- Small actions lead to big results
- Consistency is more important than intensity
- Tiny habits reduce resistance to starting
